NHBRC Education & Application

The NHBRC (National Home Builders Registration Council) offers education to help home builders understand and comply with building standards. This training is essential for ensuring quality construction and meeting legal requirements.

How to Apply

To apply for NHBRC education, you must first contact an accredited training provider, which we can assist with.

You'll then submit your application and pay the fee to enroll in the required training modules, which cover building standards, regulations, and quality control.

Upon successful completion of the training, you will receive a certificate of competence from the NHBRC.

Benefits of NHBRC Education

Enrolling in NHBRC education is essential for ensuring compliance with legal standards and for building high-quality homes.

The training provides you with industry recognition, which can lead to increased competitiveness and more business opportunities.

It also helps to mitigate the risk of legal disputes and financial penalties due to non-compliance or poor workmanship.
