About The CC

A single partner
for every business
need.

The City Consultant (The CC) is a comprehensive professional services firm headquartered in Nelspruit, Mpumalanga. We were founded on the belief that South African businesses — from startups to established enterprises — deserve access to high-quality regulatory, financial, and administrative support under one roof.

Our team brings specialist expertise across company registration, tax compliance, CIPC filings, B-BBEE certification, financial reporting, branding, and sector-specific programmes such as NHBRC. We take a client-first approach: understanding your business, removing complexity, and ensuring you operate with confidence.

Our Services

What we do

From the moment you decide to start a business to long-term compliance and growth, The City Consultant provides the professional services to keep you on solid ground.

01
Company Registration & Compliance
Full CIPC registration, statutory certificates, CSD registration, and ongoing compliance management — so your business stays legally sound from day one.
02
Tax & SARS Services
Tax clearance certificates, SARS public representative appointments, and tax affairs management to keep your relationship with SARS clean and current.
03
B-BBEE Certification
We facilitate your B-BBEE compliance verification, ensuring you hold valid certification for tendering, contracting, and regulatory requirements.
04
Financial Statements
Professionally compiled annual financial statements that meet regulatory standards and give stakeholders a clear picture of your company's financial health.
05
Business Plans
Investor-ready, funding-grade business plans crafted with strategic insight and financial rigour — built to open doors and secure capital.
06
Barcodes & Product Compliance
GS1-standard barcodes and product compliance support to get your goods retail-ready and accepted across major distribution channels.
07
CIPC Filings & Annual Returns
Beneficial ownership filings, directorship amendments, annual returns, and all CIPC-related submissions handled accurately and on time.
08
Branding & Graphic Design
Corporate identity, logo design, business profiles, and marketing materials that present your company with the professionalism it deserves.
09
Letter of Good Standing
Timely procurement of compliance certificates and letters of good standing for tenders, contracts, and government engagements.

Sector Focus

NHBRC Education
& Compliance

The National Home Builders Registration Council (NHBRC) requires home builders and contractors to be registered and compliant with national building standards. The City Consultant provides specialised support for NHBRC education, application guidance, and compliance assessment preparation.

  • Education programme enrolment through accredited training providers
  • Guidance through the NHBRC application and registration process
  • Professionally compiled Q&A preparation materials for compliance assessments
  • Ongoing support for certificate maintenance and renewals
