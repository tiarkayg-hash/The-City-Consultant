Who We Are

About The City Consultant

The City Consultant (The CC) is a comprehensive professional services firm dedicated to helping South African businesses grow with confidence.

We specialize in business registration and compliance, financial services, branding and marketing, and corporate administration — all under one roof in Nelspruit, Mpumalanga.

"Our approach is defined by precision, reliability, and a clear understanding of what it takes to build businesses that are not only compliant, but positioned to operate and expand with confidence."

Whether you're starting a new company or ensuring your existing business stays compliant across CIPC, CSD, NHBRC, COIDA, CIDB and more — we make the process seamless and efficient.
